  • Get the ingredients Maamoul are typical Syrian shortbreads, usually stuffed with dates, but which can also be filled with pistachios, almonds or walnuts.
    Traditional maamoul is made with yeasted semolina flour, farina, butter, rosewater, orange blossom water and sugar.
